Over 35 Years of Experience

Hi, my name is Barry Fisher and I'm the director of Barry Fisher Carpentry.

After leaving school at 16, I went on a YTS with my dad for 3 years then I carried on working with him for another 35 years where I got all my experience, which sadly he retired in 2020 with a bad knee. I have been self-employed for 10 years since 2010.

I have 2 NVQ`s level 3 and 4. I have made a good relationship with customers and companies and all my work is word and mouth I never have to advertise. I have a great team that work with me, Electricians and plumbers who are all qualified.

All the other work is taken on by me carpentry, plastering and tiling etc.

I have worked with some lovely customers over the years and have become good friends. Thank you for taking the time to read about me, hopefully will be working together soon.
