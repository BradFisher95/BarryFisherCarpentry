Hi, my name is Barry Fisher and I'm the director of Barry Fisher Carpentry.

I am based in Wolverhampton, West Midlands and I am a qualified tradesman with over 35 years of experience in domestic carpentry and home improvements.

After leaving school at 16, I went on a Youth Training Scheme with my dad for 3 years. Those 3 years turned into 35 years and we worked together as a family business before my dad retired in 2020 with a bad knee, being self-employed for 10 of those years.

In this time I have obtained my level 3 and 4 NVQs. I work with a great team when required including electricians and plumbers who are all qualified, with all other work for carpentry, plastering, tiling and more being taken on by me.

I have worked with some lovely customers over the years and have become good friends with many of them. As a result I have found that all my work is booked through word and mouth I have never had to advertise.

Thank you for taking the time to read about me, I hope to hear from you and that we will be working together soon.

Barry.
